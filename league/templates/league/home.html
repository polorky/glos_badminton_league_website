    Welcome!

    Welcome to the website for the Gloucestershire Badminton League!

    New to Gloucestershire? Looking for a badminton club to join?

    Head to our Clubs page for further details on the clubs that play in the Gloucestershire league.

    Introduction to the Website

    For any issues surrounding league fixtures, please contact: GlosBadFixtures@outlook.com

    For any technical issues with this website, please contact: GlosBadWebsite@gmail.com

    For any other issues surrounding the league, please contact: GlosBadCorrespondence@outlook.com

    If you need to contact other clubs, you can find general club contact details on the Clubs page and potentially team captain details on the Teams page (if the club has entered these). Note that to see this information you will need to be logged in using your club's credentials. These will have been sent to the club administrators registered with the league

    You will also need to be logged in to make changes to fixtures and submit results.
